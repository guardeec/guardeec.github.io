Welcome to the AGENCY Workshop

Hello,

We are the AGENCY team from Newcastle University. You can learn more about us and our work by visiting our website.

Our goal is to gain a deeper understanding of public opinions on the data that companies collect about individuals. We are developing an AI tool that will visualize this data and its sharing practices.

We are organizing co-creative workshops to refine this tool with your input. These workshops are open to everyone and involve discussions about data collection practices.
